Kayaking

Join us for a guided tour of the Elkhorn Slough. Our kayaking guides will take you through the heart of the slough and provide information on the area's abundant wildlife.

Hiking

Experience the beauty of Pacific Trails on one of our guided hikes. Our experienced guides will take you through the lush forests and meadows of the Santa Lucia Mountains and provide insights into the area's history and ecology.

Birdwatching

Pacific Trails is a birdwatcher's paradise. Our expert guides will take you to the best spots for birdwatching and help you identify the many species that call this area home.
