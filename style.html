Yurts at Pacific Trails

What are yurts?

Our yurts are permanent structures four meters in diameter. They have canvas walls, a wooden floor, and a clear plexiglass skylight. Heating is provided by a cleanâ€“burning propane stove. Each yurt is furnished with a queen-size bed. Fresh linens and towels are provided.

How are the yurts furnished?

Each yurt is furnished with a queen-size bed. Fresh linens and towels are provided. A table and chairs offer a beautiful setting for meals. Each yurt has a propane stove for heating and a skylight for natural lighting.

What should I bring?

Bring your personal items, food, and cooking supplies. You may also want to bring a cooler for storing food. We provide a lantern and a battery pack for light. A propane stove is provided for cooking.
